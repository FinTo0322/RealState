Welcome to the Distric Family

Distric is the new First-Hom, a company born from the partnership between one of the leading real estate companies in the Philippines, and Japanese global business enterprise, Atsuki Corporation.


With over 30 years of experience. Distric is known for its roster of noteworthy high-end developments such as The Trump Tower in Century, Dezure Urban Resort Residences and The Grabher Residences, to name a few. On the other hand, Atsuki Corporation is one of Japan’s largest conglomerates with a total of 15 business groups venturing into different industries which include Industrial Finance, Logistics & Development Group, Global Environmental & Infrastructure Business Group and Living Essentials Group, among others.


With the combined expertise of the other companies, Distric Inc. is built with distinct design, plan and quality. We believe a home is the biggest investment and most important asset of the family which is why the fulfillment of aspirations and expectations of buyers is very important to us. Distric Inc. is not just a house to live in but a legacy that can be passed on from generation to generation.


Distric Inc. focuses on the value of having your first home and believes that every homeowner deserves to take pride not only in where they live but how they live. It aims to promote a balanced life that enhances one’s way of living and overall value where they and their families can thrive in a safe and secured environment.
